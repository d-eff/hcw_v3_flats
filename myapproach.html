My Approach

I practice a holistic approach to health and wellness. Together we will look at how all areas of your life are connected, and create healthy habits to bring balance in your life.

You are your own expert.

Does stress from your job cause you to overeat? Is lack of energy affecting your relationship? My sustainable approach will get you in touch with your body's needs. I will equip you with the self=awareness to make positive changes and adapt whenever life happens.

A program tailored to YOU.

I will help you set wellness goals specific to your values and lifestyle. We'll identify steps you can take with reasonable timeframes. Instead of focusing on fad diets or counting calories, I want you to develop a deep understanding of choices that work for you.

My Training

I received my training as a Health Coach from the Institute for Integrative Nutrition. The program goes beyond traditional, clinical nutrition. It promotes the idea of holistic nourishment and creating a balance of healthy relationships, a fulfilling career, regular physical activity, and spiritual awareness.

I studied innovative coaching techniques under some of the world's top health and wellness experts. I learned cutting-edge dietary theories and lifestyle management for behavioral change and preventative health.
